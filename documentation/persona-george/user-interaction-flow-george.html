Interaction Flow

Use Case: Browsing Article Feed

Actor: George Smith

Role: Health Portal Website Registered User

Usage Preconditions:

  • George wants to read articles related to his high blood pressure.
  • George doesn't know what he wants to search for exactly.

Usage Postconditions:

  • George has found an article from browsing the feed.
  • George is reading the article.

Frequency Of Use:

  • A few days a week.

George wants to browse his health article feed.

Steps taken to browse article feed:

  1. George clicks the bookmark for the health portal website.
  2. The application responds by rendering the home page.
  3. George is already logged in - he clicks Browse articles
  4. The application looks at George's user preferences and fetches articles by keyword (his health preferences in his user data) from the API and displays the articles (card components with the image, title of article, brief description, and a link.)
  5. George scrolls through articles until he finds one that interests him and clicks the article.
  6. The application responds by opening a new tab linking to the article.
