Unregistered English Speaker Interaction Flow

Michael would navigate in his browser to our homepage. There will be an area to browse health articles, which he could
flip through there on the homepage. If he would like a specified search, there will be a search bar for him to use near
the browsing area. He would type in a subject, click a button to initiate the search, and the computer would find
articles related to his search in our database. This would also automatically take him to our health articles page. There
would be a list of articles related to his search. If he wishes to save an article upon seeing the buttons to do so near the article
titles, and he clicks on one of those buttons, the computer will prompt him to create an account on the site, and
doing this would then allow him to save articles as he chooses.
