Unregistered English Speaker Interaction Flow

Michael would navigate in his browser to our homepage. There will be an area to browse health articles, which he could
flip through there on the homepage. If he would like a specified search, there will be a search bar for him to use near
the browsing area. He would type in the subject, click a button to initiate the search, and it would bring him to our
health articles page showing articles related to his desired topic. If he wishes to save an article upon seeing the
buttons to do so under the article titles, and he clicks on one of those buttons, he would then be prompted to create an account on
the site, which would then allow him to do so.
