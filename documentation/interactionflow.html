Unregistered English Speaker Interaction Flow

  • Michael would type the URL to our homepage into his browser. His browser would load the homepage to our website.
  • There will be an area to browse health articles, which he could flip through there on the homepage. As he flips through the articles, the browser would load the articles from our database.
  • If he would like a specified search, there will be a search bar for him to use near the browsing area. He would type in a subject, click a button to initiate the search, and the computer would find articles related to his search in our database. This would also automatically take him to our health articles page.
  • There would be a list of articles related to his search. When he clicks on one, the browser will load the article from our database.
  • If he wishes to save an article upon seeing the buttons to do so near the article titles, and he clicks on one of those buttons, the computer will prompt him to create an account on the site. If he decides to do this he would be able to save articles as he chooses.
  • If he doesn't choose to make an account, he can continue looking at articles from this search, or go back to our homepage to search a different topic. To go back to the homepage, he would click a link in our navigation bar for the homepage, and the browser would load it and take him to it.
