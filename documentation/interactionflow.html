Unregistered English Speaker Interaction Flow

  • Michael would type the URL to our homepage into his browser.
  • His browser would load the homepage to our website.
  • There will be a search bar for him to use on the homepage. He would type in a subject, click a button to initiate the search.
  • The computer would find articles related to his search in our database.
  • This would also automatically take him to our health articles page.
  • There would be a list of articles related to his search and he will click on one.
  • The browser will load the article on the API site.
