Interaction Flow for a spanish user

Spanish User Preconditions

  • Santiago is determined to find a website to read and save articles for his pre diabetes.
  • Santiago wants a website where the information he is looking for is both in english and spanish.

    • Spanish User Preconditions.

  • Santiago found a website with spanish and english articles
  • Santiago reads the articles in spanish and saves them for later.
    • Santiago clicks safari on his iphone.
    • His phone loads up safari.
    • He then types on the search bar for health portal.
    • Once he gets to the website homepage, Santiago then clicks the button to translate the website into Spanish.
    • The website then translate everything to spanish.
    • Santiago then finds a button within the website that is able to let him sign in to his account.
    • Santiago browses for articles by clicking the searchbar.
    • Health portal then loads many useful articles for santiago.
    • After finding and reading a few articles Santiago then clicks the like and save button to read the article later.
    • Saved articles will show on Santiago's account feed next time he logs in.
