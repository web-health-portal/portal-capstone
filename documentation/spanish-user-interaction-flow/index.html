Interaction Flow for a spanish user

  • Santiago search for health portal, his favorite health website.
  • Once he gets to the website homepage, Santiago then clicks the button to translate the website into Spanish.
  • He then finds a button within the website that is able to let him sign in to his account.
  • Santiago then types his email address and password.
  • Santiago then uses the search bar to look for more valuable information relating to his pre diabetes condition.
  • After finding and reading a few articles Santiago then clicks the like and save button to read the article later.
